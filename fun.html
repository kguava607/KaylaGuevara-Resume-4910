Chocolate Chip Cookies

View Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix butter, sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
  3. Add eggs and mix well.
  4. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes.

Banana Bread

View Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mash bananas in a bowl.
  3. Mix in melted butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla.
  4. Add baking soda, salt, and flour.
  5. Pour into a loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes.
