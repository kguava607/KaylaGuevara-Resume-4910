Tomato Basil Soup

A homemade soup recipe I enjoy making.

View Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs of roma tomatoes
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 3/4 cup of fresh basil
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup of 2% milk
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and cook until soft.
  3. Add garlic and cook for about 30 seconds.
  4. Pour in crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth.
  5. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 15 to 20 minutes.
  6. Stir in cream and fresh basil.
  7. Blend if you want a smoother texture.
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste, then serve warm.
